Quirk 01

Atemporality

LLMs don't experience time linearly. They learn a static snapshot of the world based on when their training data was scraped — forever frozen at their knowledge cutoff. Ask the same question across different training windows and you get entirely different "current" realities.

Quirk 02

Uniformity

Humans hold a rich spectrum of opinions forming a natural bell curve. LLMs optimize for the most statistically probable token, collapsing that messy human diversity into a single, authoritative spike — the mode of all human expression.

Quirk 03

Social Acceptability Bias

To make AI safe for consumers, labs tune out toxicity via RLHF safety fine-tuning. For sociologists simulating real, gritty human interaction, this creates a sanitized world that never existed — all surface polish, no raw humanity.
